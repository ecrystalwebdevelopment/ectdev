About
VSSS Alumni Association

The Vijayanta Model Higher Secondary School Alumni Association (VSSS Alumni) is a proud and vibrant community of former students from both Vijayanta Senior Secondary School and Vijayanta Model Higher Secondary School, united by lasting bonds and cherished memories of our beloved institutions.

The association was established in 2008 and formally registered under the Tamil Nadu Societies Registration Act, 1975 with Registration No. 236/2009.

Since its formation, the association has actively connected alumni from various batches, fostered lifelong friendships, and supported initiatives contributing to the growth, welfare, and lasting legacy of both schools. Functioning with the guidance and concerns of the HVF Management and both school administrations, the association continues to serve as a platform for alumni to reunite, celebrate their shared history, and give back to the Vijayanta educational family.

About
VSSS Alumni Association

The VSSS Alumni Association (VSSS Alumni) is a proud, vibrant community of former students from both Vijayanta Senior Secondary School and Vijayanta Model Higher Secondary School. United by lasting bonds, shared traditions, and cherished memories of our beloved alma maters, we continue to celebrate the spirit and legacy of Vijayanta.

Established in 2008, the association has been actively fostering connections among alumni while maintaining a strong rapport with the schools and their management. In 2009, the association was formally registered under the Tamil Nadu Societies Registration Act, 1975 (Registration No. 236/2009), further solidifying our commitment to unity, growth, and community service.

Through various events, reunions, mentorship programs, and collaborative initiatives, VSSS Alumni Association serves as a platform for former students to reconnect, support one another, and contribute to the continuous progress of our schools and society.
